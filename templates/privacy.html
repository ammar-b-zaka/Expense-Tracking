Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic session information needed to keep you logged in.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what is necessary to run the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To display your expenses and spending summaries within the app.
  • To authenticate your identity and keep your account secure.
  • To send essential service communications (e.g., password resets).

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

While we implement reasonable security measures, no system is completely immune to risk. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties. We may use minimal infrastructure services (e.g., hosting providers) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you at any time. You can manage most of this directly within the app, or contact us to request assistance.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app.
